About me

Hedda Petersson

My name is Hedda Petersson, and I am a passionate Graphic Design and Web Development student at Jönköping University. My love for design stems from a lifelong appreciation for creativity and visual expression. I attended art school for nine years, where I honed my artistic skills and developed a deep interest in design.

In 2022, I had the opportunity to take graphic design classes at Santa Barbara City College in California, USA. This experience allowed me to expand my skills and gain a deeper understanding of design principles and techniques.

I am driven by a desire to create meaningful and impactful designs, combining aesthetics with functionality. My goal is to continue growing as a creative professional and contributing innovative solutions in both graphic design and web development.
