Coffee table-book

MADE IN ADOBE INDESIGN - FALL 24


This coffee table book celebrates the legendary Danish designer Hans J. Wegner, renowned for his timeless, functional furniture. Designed in Adobe InDesign, the book features high-quality images of his iconic pieces. The layout uses minimalist design principles, reflecting Wegner's own aesthetic, with clean typography and visually captivating spreads. This book not only honors Wegner’s legacy but also serves as a beautiful object in itself, embodying the harmony between form and function.
